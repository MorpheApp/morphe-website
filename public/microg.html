What is MicroG?

MicroG is a lightweight, open-source reimplementation of Google Play Services. It provides the essential APIs that YouTube Morphe and YT Music Morphe need to function without the privacy concerns and resource drain of official Google services.

Core Technical Pillars

dataset GmsCore

The primary component that handles account authentication and push notifications (FCM), letting you sign in securely.

location_on UnifiedNlp

A privacy-first location provider that lets apps determine position without reporting data to Google.

account_tree Service Framework Proxy

A small helper that allows apps to believe they are running on an official Google-certified device.

Why Morphe needs it

login

Google Account Auth

Without MicroG, modded YouTube apps cannot connect to Google's authentication servers. MicroG acts as the secure bridge.

notifications_active

Cloud Messaging

Enables real-time push notifications for new uploads and community posts via Google's Firebase Cloud Messaging.

Quick Start Guide

1

Download & Install

Download the latest MicroG GmsCore APK from our official releases and install it like any regular app.

2

Sign Into Google

Open the MicroG Settings app on your device, tap 'Add Account', and sign in with your Google credentials.

3

Enjoy Morphe

Open YouTube Morphe or YT Music Morphe. You are now ready to enjoy a fully connected experience.
